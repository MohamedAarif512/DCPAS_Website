System Flow

How DCPAS Works

Three coordinated steps keep your farm protected around the clock.

Step 1

Detect

PIR motion sensors and ultrasonic detectors identify wild boar activity at the farm perimeter. The ESP-32 processes sensor data in real-time.

Step 2

Deter

Upon detection, the system activates multi-layered deterrents — loud buzzers emit alarming frequencies while water sprays create a physical barrier.

Step 3

Alert

The distributed nodes communicate wirelessly and push instant notifications to the farmer's phone, logging every intrusion event for review.
