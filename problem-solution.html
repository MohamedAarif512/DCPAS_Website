The Problem

Crops Destroyed Overnight

Wild boars raid farms under cover of darkness, destroying months of work in a single night. By the time a farmer discovers the damage, it is already too late.

Traditional Deterrents Fail

Static electric fences, scarecrows, and loud noises all habituate over time. Boars learn to ignore them within days, rendering expensive investments useless.

Manual Guarding is Expensive

Hiring night watchmen to patrol farm perimeters is costly, unsustainable, and still leaves large areas uncovered. Human fatigue means gaps in protection.

No Real-Time Awareness

Farmers have no way of knowing an intrusion is happening until they physically inspect their land. By then, damage is already done and the boars are long gone.

Lethal Methods Cause Legal & Ethical Issues

Trapping and culling wild boars is heavily regulated, ethically controversial, and often illegal. Farmers who resort to lethal methods risk fines and legal action.

The DCPAS Solution

24/7 Automated Perimeter Watch

ESP-32 nodes with PIR sensors and cameras monitor every corner of your farm continuously — day and night — without any human intervention required.

Multi-Layered Dynamic Deterrence

DCPAS varies its deterrent response — combining randomised buzzer frequencies with water spray bursts — preventing habituation and keeping deterrents effective long-term.

Zero Ongoing Labour Cost

Once installed, DCPAS operates fully autonomously. No staff required. The distributed mesh network self-coordinates, covering the entire farm perimeter automatically.

Instant Mobile Alerts in Under 10 Seconds

The moment a boar is detected, DCPAS pushes a notification directly to the farmer's phone — including the location of the intrusion — in under 10 seconds.

100% Non-Lethal, Fully Legal

Every component of DCPAS deters without harming. Sound and water cause no injury to wildlife, making it fully compliant with wildlife protection regulations everywhere.
