Our Story

Built to Protect
Every Harvest

DCPAS was created out of a real need — to give farmers an intelligent, humane, and affordable way to protect their crops from wild boar invasions.

Mission & Vision

Why We Built DCPAS

Wild boar intrusions cause massive crop losses every year. We set out to build a solution that is smart, sustainable, and 100% non-lethal.

The Problem

Farmers Lose Millions to Wild Boars

Wild boar attacks on farmland are a growing global crisis. Traditional deterrents like fences and traps are expensive, ineffective, or inhumane — leaving farmers helpless against recurring crop destruction.

Our Solution

Intelligent, Distributed Defense

DCPAS deploys a mesh network of ESP-32 powered smart nodes across your farm perimeter. Each node detects, deters, and alerts — working together automatically, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

Our Mission

Technology That Works for Farmers

We believe every farmer deserves access to cutting-edge protection technology. DCPAS is designed to be affordable, easy to install, and reliable in the toughest field conditions.

Our Vision

Farms Protected, Wildlife Respected

We're committed to building solutions that protect livelihoods without harming wildlife. Every component of DCPAS is designed to deter, not hurt — because humans and nature can coexist.
